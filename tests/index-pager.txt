--------------------------------------------------
INDEX

Open mailbox - view index
  A01) select email (cursor up/down)
  A02) jump to email (numbers)
  A03) sort emails

Receive new email in current mailbox
  A04) before current email
  A05) after current email

Receive old email in current mailbox
  A06) before current email
  A07) after current email

Misc
  A08) Receive new email in different mailbox
  A09) Receive old email in different mailbox
  A10) sent email saved to current mailbox
  A11) sent email saved to different mailbox

--------------------------------------------------
INDEX (limited)

  B01-11) repeat A01-11 with a limit in effect

--------------------------------------------------
PAGER (pager_index_lines = 0)

Open email - view pager
  C01) select email (cursor up/down)
  C02) jump to email (numbers)
  C03) sort emails
  C04) move up on first email
  C05) move down on last email

Receive new email in current mailbox
  C06) before current email
  C07) after current email

Receive old email in current mailbox
  C08) before current email
  C09) after current email

Misc
  C10) Receive new email in different mailbox
  C11) Receive old email in different mailbox
  C12) sent email saved to current mailbox
  C13) sent email saved to different mailbox

--------------------------------------------------
PAGER (limited, pager_index_lines = 0)

  D01-13) repeat C01-13 with a limit in effect

--------------------------------------------------

ATTACHMENT

Open pager when viewing an attachment
  G01) new mail in this mailbox
  G02) old mail in this mailbox
  G03) new mail in other mailbox
  G04) old mail in other mailbox
